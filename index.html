campus life

school library

Established on 1998

The Central Library and the library at the Pre-primary section play a key role in arousing curiosity, understanding and inculcate reading habit in students, thereby scaffolding inquiry learning. Both the libraries are an organised collection of information. A well qualified Librarian is also present here to help the students in selecting appropriate books.

school auditorium

established on 2005

The seminar hall on the second floor of Arena gives facility to conduct enrichment programmes, workshops, seminars or lectures for a larger audience. It is equipped with projectors, lighting arrangement and comfortable seating. A technical expert is also there to render help during the presentations.

Narendra Damodardas Modi (Gujarati: [ˈnəɾendɾə dɑmodəɾˈdɑs ˈmodiː] (About this soundlisten); born 17 September 1950)[a] is an Indian politician serving as the 14th and current prime minister of India since 2014. He is also a member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a Hindu nationalist volunteer organisation. He is the first prime minister born after India's independence in 1947, the second non-Congress one to win two consecutive terms after Atal Bihari Vajpayee and the first from outside the Congress to win both terms with a majority in the Lok Sabha.
